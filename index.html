SCRIPTLESS

I conducted a survey in class asking people about their public speaking skils 40% of respondents (45 in total) said they were either not confident or somewhat not confident. Despite this 96% of them publicly spoke frequently or more. This means that a large portion are leaving bad impressions when forced to publicly speak. When asked how people prepared the most common answer by far was people wrote a script. SCRIPTLESS was born. A web app to help you write, remember and practice your scripts.

check it out here:

https://interactivedesign.ca/~bwebster/
